Grilled Cheese

ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 2-3 oz of grated sharp cheddar cheese
  • Butter

instructions

  1. Start by grating your cheddar cheese and buttering the bottom of one slice of your sourdough bread.
  2. Assemble the sandwich while also heating up any non-stick pan over medium high heat.
  3. Apply a small amount of butter to the pan and waiting for the butter to slightly foam, at which point it’ll be ready to drop your sandwich in.
  4. Using any flat surface like a spatula, press down on the grilled cheese until one side gets a nice golden-brown color. Once your grilled cheese is ready to flip butter the top side of the grilled cheese with more butter.
  5. Flip the grilled cheese and repeat the process of pressing down on it with a flat surface until it’s ready.
