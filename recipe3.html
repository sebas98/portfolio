Chicken Parmesan

ingredients

  • One chicken breast (butterflied in half)
  • 4oz of low moisture mozzarella cheese
  • 5oz tomato sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 oz grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 sprig of basil (for garnish)
  • Extra virgin olive oil

instructions

  1. Cover both butterflied chicken breasts with plastic wrap and proceed to pound them into they are thin (not too much or else they’ll become tattered)
  2. Crack and whisk one egg in a separate bowl and dip each chicken breast into the egg mixture, then pressing them into your breadcrumbs, coating the chicken completely.
  3. Heat a non-stick or cast-iron pan over medium high heat with your olive oil until the oil is shimmering but not smoking hot.
  4. Place the chicken breasts into the pan and using a spoon baste the oil onto the side that isn’t frying to help it brown faster once flipped.
  5. Once one side is golden brown flip to the other side and wait until the other side is also visibly done.
  6. Preheat your oven or broiler to 450 degrees and on a rimmed baking sheet place each chicken breast with your tomato sauce on top and the mozzarella cheese. Broil/bake for 5 minutes or until you see the cheese is melted properly.
  7. Chiffonade your basil and chop to sprinkle on top of the finished chicken in addition with your reserved parmesan cheese.
